Brief introduction of children’s development character age from 0 to 6

Below 1 year old babyhood (corresponding age: Below 1 year old)

Mind Features

The stage of discovery and exploration. They belong to the age of intuitive thinking, and are only interested in things that are around them. They are in the process of hearing beautiful music and kind words from their parents, as well as developing their enjoyment of listening to adults read them stories and children's songs.

 

Key Ability Training

Body control ability: learning to stand, sit, crawl, and other simple body controls.

Exploration Skills: knocking, beating, random illustrations etc.

Language skills: from pronunciation word by word, they gradually developed to say  polysyllabic words (such as father and mother).

Encyclopedia knowledge & integrated capacities: understanding family, facial features, limbs, some common fruits, several common daily items.

 

1 -2 years old (corresponding age: 1 year old)

Mind Features

The stage of discovery and exploration. They are only interested in their own surroundings. They begin to have significant memories and be in language infancy stage when they are about 1 years old. In that condition, they like painting and imitations. Their curiosity and thirst for knowledge are enhanced during that period. Also they would generate strong interest in Chinese characters, reading, listening to songs or music, stories, as well as perform with their body language. They can sing simple songs and make certain movements. They can even understand and execute simple prescriptive language.

 

Key Ability Training

Action Coordination Ability: gradually learn to walk steadily, brisk walking, running and other activities; imitation exercise; painting, and will draw simple horizontal and vertical lines.

Self-care ability: eat with spoon; drink with cup; dress and undress, etc.

Language ability: aged 1-1.5 years old: say basic words; 1.5-2 years old: say their name; make simple sentences with a simple answer; literacy; imitate animal noises.

Mathematical ability: rhythm and rhyme; recognize 1-2 kinds of graphics; learn to count.

Encyclopedia knowledge, integrated capacities: recognize several common natural phenomenon’s; some common fruits, vegetables, small animals, common means of transportation, daily necessities; recognize several common colors.

 

2 -3 years old (corresponding age: 2 year old)

Mind Features

Human-specific language, imagination and thinking are formed at about 2 years old. About 2 years of age is a key time in the development. It’s the most likely period to accept adult education, and can comply with simple rules and requirements. 2-3 years is the critical period of language development. They are particularly fond of speaking, and form the basic ability of understanding and speaking. At that time, building of self-awareness, a strong emotional attachment and a clear self-centered tendency are typical for them. An emergence of their first life revolt appears; they have volatile emotion, resist parental rules, or show aggressive behavior. At the same time, they have an independent tendency, and enjoy labor. They also like to repeat and imitate, they have a good memory, and can quickly recite a song or poem.

 

Key Ability Training

Action Coordination Ability: jump in place, running freely, construction, and other activities. Simple instrumental activities such as clicking fingers, clapping hands, cycling, wearing beads; holding a pen correctly, painting,  imitation etc.

Social skills: cooperating with other children. Enjoys singing, music games, dancing .

Self-care ability: wear socks, wash hands, eat, putting on and taking off clothes, but limited by the development of motor skills, they still need adult help on their self-care behavior sometime.

Language ability: 2-2.5 years: a single sentence, to understand the story, and be polite; 2.5-3 years old: can complete complex sentences and use adjectives. They would recite very few complete songs. We could exercise children's spoken language by offering a variety of books for young children, homemade stories, songs, pictures and other facilities. Literacy.

Mathematic skills: Understanding 3 to 6 types of geometry; usually a kid aged 2 years old can count from  10 to 40; point from 5 to 10; know 1-10, usually, child aged 2 can recognize 2-4 digits.

Encyclopedia knowledge, integrated capacities: can name most of the body parts; has learnt several more common natural phenomenon’s; has learnt more about some common fruits, vegetables, small animals and plants, common means of transportation, daily necessities, recognize and identify several colors.

 

3 -4 years old (corresponding age: 3 year old)

Mind Features

Unintentional memory or mechanical memory, are the main types of Memory. Poor ability to distinguish right from wrong, have figurative thinking and conscious behavior has begun to develop. At a critical period of language development, language has developed faster and the child has become more talkative. They are interested in bright color, and they love to imitate. They are still in the "first revolt", the first big emotional effect, occurring when the child is 3 and 4 years old. They are prone to aggressive behavior and are still full of imagination. At about 4 years old, they are in a critical period of reading enlightenment and the golden age of gaming activities.

 

Key Ability Training

Action Coordination Ability: painting with pens, walking up and down stairs freely, actively avoid collision with others when running.

Social skills: like role-playing games; learn how to be the leader, divide up labor; interested in new experiences and more independent.

Self-care ability: brush their teeth, wash their face, tie shoes and so on; imitate parents doing housework.

Language Skills: vocabulary at highest point of growth; learning the easiest antonyms; will say "thank you", "Sorry" and some polite language, can speak long complex sentences; understand pictures, literacy, and read picture stories.

Mathematic skills: can understand and write the numbers 1- 10, sometimes they may create the  wrong sequence or direction in writing words; draw points, lines, circles; can count numbers to up to 50 and point to 12 or 13; can read double-digits; classification; know addition below 5; clock watching becomes habit.

Encyclopedia knowledge, integrated capacities: differentiate gender; distinguish red, yellow, green, black and white; Recognize the common animals and plants, pay attention to and find the variety of plants and animals around; they can perceive how the weather effects their lives and activities; can sense the difference between soft, hard, smooth, and other characteristics of objects and materials; understanding more common fruits, vegetables, small animals, common means of transportation  and daily necessities.

 

4 -5 years old (corresponding age: 4 year old)

Mind Features

Kids are transited from direct action thinking to concrete thinking since turning 4, and the intended memory or mechanical memory are still main types of memory. They learn quickly but also forgot fast. Building of awareness and sense of order, right and wrong is vague, some skills like communication and cognition are improved, they prefer asking questions, and began to have a sense of responsibility. They can classify according to the surface properties of things as there is understanding enhanced. Manifestations and expression forms are diversified during the critical period of writing.

Key Ability

Action Coordination Ability: painting, clay sculpture, playing hula hoop, jump rope, etc; drawing triangles, trapezoid, animals, people and goods; cutting paper strips, building blocks and other fine motor training.

Social competence: prefer to please the junior partners, there may be a "best" friend. Enjoy dancing and gymnastics. Their conduct is more independent. They may do things such as visit next door neighbor by themselves, recognize directions and lead the way etc.

Self-care ability: buttoning up clothes, zipping, fold quilts, sorting toys, pour themselves a drink and so on.

Language skills: like to sing simple songs. They can complete actions according to adult instruction. Like simple, short stories and could summarize others’ words and stories.

Mathematic skills: able to tell three kinds of shapes. Recognize the clock, the same number of points and then recite, will do continuous addition below 5, a few will even add to 10. They can subtract below 10 and a few will be able to subtract below 20. Could read multiple digits, and do addition. Will count backwards by 2 digits, and even half of them could count backwards by 3 digits. Classify according to shape and color. They can follow simple rhythms.

Encyclopedia knowledge, integrated capacities: can name 8 to 10 colors. Found that plants and animals change in growth, as well as its basic conditions; can perceive, find simple physical phenomena, such as changes in body shape or position; the characteristics of different seasons; commonly used technology products and its relations with their own lives; know more common fruits, vegetables, small plants and animals, common means of transportation and daily necessities.

 

5 -6 years old  (corresponding age: 5、6 year old )

Mind Features

Development of abstract thinking, language skills, self-care ability and work capacity has improved significantly. Love learning, asking questions and have a strong thirst for knowledge. The initial formation of personality. Gradually forms the awareness of rules, the sense of cooperation, the enhancement of teamwork, particularly interested in competitive games and multiplayer games. Enhanced sense of responsibility and emotional regulation, a significant enhancement in reading (high motivation for improved literacy, strong memory), like complex story plots. They can classify according to the properties of things. They are in the critical period of writing. When they are 5-years-old, baby's interest in learning a foreign language is significantly higher than other age groups.

Key Ability training

Social competence: courtesy; to comply with the basic norms in group activities; will call people in different occupations (such as teachers, doctors); understand cooperation and sharing; emjoy dancing, musical instruments, paintings, performances etc.

Self-care ability: dress and undress quickly and neatly; wash hands and be hygienic, etc; also gradually teach them to do some simple household chores, such as: sweeping, wiping tables and so on.

Languages: the right way to pronounce various syllables, and master a large quantity of vocabulary, can communicate with others relatively well. Like consistent story with plot, they can repeat, play or perform a complete or partial story.

Mathematical ability: they can classify according to each type of things (such as fruits, plants, etc.); recognition of direction; recognize banknotes, coins etc; recognize rulers, see scales; learn to paint squares, ovals; learn to look at the clock and read the correct time. Recognize and read 4-5 digits; count backwards with 4 to 6 digits;

Encyclopedic knowledge, integrated capacities: able to identify 10 common colors; understand more common fruits, vegetables, animals, plants and the common means of transportation; able to be aware of the adaption of shape characteristics and habits of plants and animals to their living environment; perception and understanding the close relationship  between other people and the natural environment, the impact of common severe weather to people's production and life; and knowing to respect and cherish life, and protect the environment.
