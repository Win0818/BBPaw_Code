Dad and Mom, Where Are We Going for Summer Vacation?

Wow! Dad and Mom, where shall we go for 60 days summer vacation? Look! I want to go to the children’s playground with you!

1. HK Disneyland

Age Group: 4-12 years old

As our closest Disney, HK Disneyland is the best choice for family vacation! There are 7 themes in HK Disneyland: American Town Street, Adventureland, Fantasyland, Tomorrowland, Toy Story Camp, Grizzly Bear Valley and Mystic Manor. Grizzly Bear Valley and Mystic Manor are both unique to HK Disneyland! An advantage of HK Disneyland is that every scenic spot is centralized, so they can play all day, and it will not be too hard for parents and baby!

2. Singapore Sentosa Island Super Paradise

Age Group: 5-6 years old

If a family wants to choose a comfortable tourist destination, Singapore Sentosa Island Super Paradise will be absolutely the best choice. Sentosa Island not only includes famous integrated resorts, but also has theme parks, a variety of wildlife centers, adventure parks, restaurants, a spa and so on. Whether it is the excitement of exploring nature, understanding tropical natural worlds, or closely connecting with the incredible animals in Butterfly Garden and insect museum, it is an edutainment novelty style. The marine life park should not be missed, as it is known as the world’s largest aquarium. Children can not only dive into the mysterious sea world to enjoy the colorful marine life, but also play with dolphins, interact with colorful marine life in a shallow pool or expand their knowledge and see rare coral.

3. Thailand Tree Tale

Age Group: 3-15 years old

At the location of a luxury resort in Thailand named SonevaKir, Koh Kood is located in the Gulf of Siam, with fine sand and blue sea. For children, SonevaKir is an adventure park full of fairy tale. Edutainment is a special theme prepared for kids.

Architects built a tree in fairy kindom named The Den, in the dense rainforest. The Den is an overhead bamboo frame structure, and its appearance is like giant manta rays hovering over the jungle. Huge internal space provide the concept of exciting activities formed around “LIFE” (namely learning, inspiration, fun and experience). The Den is located in the tree. Its shape looks like giant manta rays, and includes an art room, music room, fashion room and a library, inside. Like a sailing boat in the tree, the library has a glass window from which plants and trees can be observed.

To be frank, Dad and Mom, where to go is not important, staying with you is!
